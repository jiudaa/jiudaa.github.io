Alphapose安装

2021-05-15 | 姿态估计

说明

nvidia 10.0
安装之前我升级了显卡 从9.0->10.0

python scripts/demo_inference.py –cfg D:/Projects/PoseTracking/AlphaPose/configs/coco/resnet/256x192_res152_lr1e-3_1x-duc.yaml –checkpoint D:/Projects/PoseTracking/AlphaPose/fast_421_res152_256x192.pth –video D:/Projects/alphapose/data/adata/Con201/1A.wmv –outdir examples/res –pose_flow –save_video

如果显存不够，可在后面调整epoch：```--detbatch 1 --posebatch 30```
window下的kill：```taskkill /F /PID pid```
