Competition and Data Description

  • Competition description: Human BioMolecular Atlas Program (HuBMAP) is working on the development of a framework for mapping the human body at a level of glomeruli functional tissue units. The goal of this competition “Hacking the Kidney” is to detect functional tissue units (FTUs) across different tissue preparation pipelines and implement a successful and robust glomeruli FTU detector.
  • Data description:
  1. Training dataset: 15 tiff images with masks.
  2. Testing dataset: 5 tiff images.

    Totally 30.71GB including training and testing data.
  3. Evaluation: Dice coefficient
  • Difficulties: there is some software that does automatic or semi-automatic labeling first, then there are some expert to verify or correct the initial annotations
